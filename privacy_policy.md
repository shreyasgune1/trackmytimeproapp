# Privacy Policy for TrackMyTime PRO
Effective Date: April 17, 2026
Last Updated: April 17, 2026

## 1. Our Privacy Philosophy
TrackMyTime PRO is built on a "Privacy-First" architecture. We believe your time is your own, and so is your data. The application is designed to function fully offline and does not require or collect any personal information from you.

## 2. Information Collection and Use
### A. Personal Information
**No Collection**: TrackMyTime PRO does not require account creation, registration, or any form of identification. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers.

**No Third-Party SDKs**: We do not use third-party analytics (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics) or advertising SDKs. Your app usage patterns are never tracked or transmitted to third parties.

### B. App Data & Local Storage
**Local Processing**: All data you enter into the app—including task names, project descriptions, and time logs—is stored exclusively in a private database on your mobile device. 

**No Cloud Transmission**: No data entered into the app is transmitted to our servers or any cloud provider. Your data remains under your physical control at all times.

## 3. Google Play Data Safety Compliance
In accordance with Google Play's 2026 Data Safety requirements:
- **Data Collection**: We declare that our app does not collect or share any user data.
- **Data Sharing**: We do not share any data with third-party companies or organizations.
- **Security Practices**: Since no data is transmitted to the cloud, there is no data in transit to encrypt.

## 4. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)
Since we do not collect, store, or process any personal information, your rights under the CCPA/CPRA (including the right to access, delete, or opt-out of the sale of personal information) are inherently protected.
- **Access & Portability**: You can export your entire time-tracking history into a JSON or PDF format at any time directly from the app’s Analytics section for your own records.

## 5. Permissions
TrackMyTime PRO operates with minimal system permissions. 
- **Storage**: Used only to save your exported PDF reports and JSON backups to your device's Downloads folder.
- **Note**: We do not request access to your Contacts, Location, Camera, Microphone, or Identity.

## 6. Data Deletion
Since all data is stored locally, you have total control over its deletion. You can wipe all data by:
1. Clearing the App Data/Cache in your Android System Settings.
2. Uninstalling the application.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding this policy or our privacy practices:
Email: support@trackmytimepro.com
